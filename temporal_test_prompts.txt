=== TEMPORAL CONSTRAINT TEST PROMPTS ===
(Ordered from easiest to hardest)

1. EASY - Simple Lookahead
---
Backtest AAPL from 2020-01-01 to 2024-01-01.
Enter long when the 10-day moving average crosses above the 50-day moving average and within the next 5 trading days the 20-day realized volatility drops below its 1-year median.
Exit when the 10-day moving average crosses below the 50-day moving average.

2. EASY - Simple Duration
---
Backtest TSLA from 2019-01-01 to 2023-01-01.
Enter long when the 5-day moving average crosses above the 20-day moving average.
Exit when the 5-day moving average has been below the 20-day moving average for 3 consecutive days.

3. MEDIUM - Sequential Entry
---
Backtest GOOGL from 2020-01-01 to 2024-01-01.
Enter long if first the 10-day moving average crosses above the 50-day moving average, and then within the next 3 trading days the 20-day realized volatility drops below its 1-year median.
If the volatility condition doesn't happen within 3 days, do not enter.
Exit when the 10-day moving average crosses below the 50-day moving average.

4. MEDIUM - Entry Duration + Exit Lookahead
---
Backtest MSFT from 2021-01-01 to 2024-01-01.
Enter long when the 10-day moving average has been above the 50-day moving average for 2 straight days.
Exit when the 10-day moving average crosses below the 50-day moving average within the next 2 trading days.

5. HARD - Multiple Sequential Rules
---
Backtest NVDA from 2020-01-01 to 2024-01-01.
Enter long if first the 10-day moving average crosses above the 50-day moving average, then within the next 5 trading days the 20-day realized volatility drops below its 1-year median, and then within the next 2 trading days after that the 5-day moving average crosses above the 10-day moving average.
Exit when the 10-day moving average crosses below the 50-day moving average.

6. HARD - Sequential Entry + Duration Exit
---
Backtest AMZN from 2019-01-01 to 2023-01-01.
Enter long if first the 20-day moving average crosses above the 50-day moving average, and then within the next 4 trading days the 30-day realized volatility drops below its 1-year median.
Exit when the 20-day moving average has been below the 50-day moving average for 2 consecutive days.

7. VERY HARD - Complex Sequential with Multiple Constraints
---
Backtest SPY from 2020-01-01 to 2024-01-01.
Enter long if first the 10-day moving average crosses above the 50-day moving average, then within the next 3 trading days the 20-day realized volatility must drop below its 1-year median, and then within the next 2 trading days after that the 5-day moving average must cross above the 10-day moving average.
If any of these conditions don't happen within their time windows, do not enter.
Exit when the 10-day moving average has been below the 50-day moving average for 2 straight days.
